TimeBox

TimeBox is an online tool for sketching and sharing instrumental music.

All song data is contained in the URL at the top of your browser. When you make changes, the URL updates. Copy it to share!

is appreciated!

Instructions

Add/remove notes by clicking the gray rows. Patterns are edited via the numbered boxes at the bottom.

  • Spacebar: play/pause
  • Z/Y: undo/redo
  • C/V: copy/paste
